Limiting Russia's Propaganda

Putin's war on Ukraine hinges on the support of the Russian public. Together we can inhibit the spread of propaganda created by the Russian Government.

By running the program on this website, you are actively participating in disturbing Russian state-influenced media. Once you press "Start" your browser will continuously send requests to the sites shown in the table below. With enough participants, the sites will eventually get overloaded and won't be reachable anymore.

Our goal is to encourage the Russian people to seek out other news sources. This website is part of a bigger, coordinated and ongoing effort to disable Putin's propaganda machine.

Find the code to this website on github.

⚠️ Before you start

  • Use a VPN to obscure your location! Participating in this type of activism (ddos) is illegal. Proton VPN offers a base version of their VPN for free here.

  • Do not use this program over mobile broadband. This website will repeatedly send requests to various Russian news outlets and will deplete data-plans quickly.
